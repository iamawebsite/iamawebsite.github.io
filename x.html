February 3, 2024: Adelphi University Chabad announces that it will bring Sam Fried, an Israeli Defense Forces soldier, to Adelphi for an event called "Inspiration from an IDF Soldier." Adelphi Students for Justice in Palestine brings attention to the event on Instagram, asking "What inspiration do Adelphi students need from a man who is participating in the mass murder of civilians?"

February 4, 2024: Adelphi University students contribute signatures to a strongly worded petition asking administration to shut down this event.

February 5, 2024: Adelphi Students for Justice in Palestine announces on Instagram that administration has told them, "Academic freedom includes protections for those at an instituiton who speak and explore their percieved truths..."

February 6, 2024: "Inspiration from an IDF Soldier" goes on as planned, with Adelphi Students for Justice in Palestine protesting outside the recreation center. Adelphi Public Safety and Garden City Police Department observe, and tell journalists from The Delphian they can only have one photographer. Sam Fried leaves campus unharmed and presumably returns to Gaza, he is cited by Chabad as being back in the USA only temporarily.

April 15, 2024: The Delphian covers the protest in their print newspaper, citing accounts from both Jewish and Palestinian students that they were harassed the day of the protest.

April 29, 2024: Adelphi Students for Justice in Palestine protest for divestment from all companies aiding Israel.
